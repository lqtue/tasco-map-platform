Job Purpose

The POI / Geocoding / Search Operations Specialist owns the quality, completeness, and usability of the place and address data that powers Tasco Maps, MyTasco, VETC mobility services, local search, discovery, routing destinations, and vehicle-related commerce.

This role is responsible for building and maintaining high-quality datasets for points of interest, addresses, entrances, access points, administrative hierarchy, search ranking signals, and geofences.

The role is not only about maintaining POIs. It is about building a Vietnam-localized place intelligence system for maps, navigation, parking, fuel, EV charging, toll stations, vehicle services, insurance, logistics, and local commerce.

Key Responsibilities

A. POI Data Quality Governance
  • Own and define POI data-quality standards for Tasco Maps across priority regions: Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City, expressways, national highways, VETC/Tasco locations, parking sites, fuel stations, EV charging stations, car service centers, and toll stations.
  • Design and maintain POI classification taxonomy, naming standards, category hierarchy, address format, opening hours, phone number, website, payment method, brand, chain ID, and operational status.
  • Define minimum data requirements per POI category: display name, category, address, lat/lng, entrance/access point, road-side access direction, business status, source, freshness, and validation status.
  • Lead large-scale POI cleaning, deduplication, merging, normalization, and enrichment workflows.
  • Monitor POI completeness, duplication rate, wrong-location rate, stale data rate, and category accuracy by city, district, and business category.
B. Geocoding & Address Intelligence
  • Own the quality of forward and reverse geocoding datasets for Vietnamese addresses.
  • Build address normalization rules for Vietnam-specific structures: province/city, ward/commune, street, alley/lane/ngo/ngach/hem, building, house number, apartment, floor, and unit.
  • Improve address-to-coordinate accuracy for residential addresses, business addresses, VETC/Tasco service points, logistics pickup/dropoff, parking entrances, and toll station entrances.
  • Establish accuracy metrics: rooftop-level accuracy, street-level accuracy, ward-level accuracy, wrong-side-of-road rate, entrance mismatch rate, and failed geocode rate.
C. POI Search & Discovery Quality
  • Own search quality for POI and place discovery in Tasco Maps, MyTasco, VETC, and related mobility applications.
  • Define relevance metrics: top-1 accuracy, top-3 accuracy, click-through rate, zero-result rate, wrong-result rate, category precision, distance-to-intent, and reroute-after-search rate.
  • Work with Search/AI Engineering to improve autocomplete, fuzzy matching, Vietnamese accent-insensitive search, abbreviation handling, brand search, category search, nearby search, and search ranking.
  • Maintain query dictionaries for Vietnamese mobility contexts: parking, EV charging, fuel stations, car wash, toll station, vehicle inspection, garage, roadside assistance, insurance, VETC, and Tasco.
D. POI Geofence & Polygon Governance
  • Define and maintain geofences for operational and commercial use cases: parking zones, toll station zones, service coverage areas, dealership catchment areas, restricted service zones, logistics pickup/dropoff zones, and high-demand mobility zones.
  • Own the spatial quality of POI polygons and geofences: geometry validity, polygon overlap, containment, entrance/exit points, road access, service-area boundaries, and point-in-polygon accuracy.
  • Define geofence KPIs: false inclusion rate, false exclusion rate, polygon freshness, business-rule compliance, and geofence-trigger accuracy.
E. Data Sources, Conflation & Validation
  • Integrate and validate data from OpenStreetMap, Overture Maps, government datasets, Tasco/VETC internal business data, partner data, field collection, street imagery, user contributions, and business-owner submissions.
  • Design POI conflation rules to merge duplicates. Establish evidence requirements for POI validation: image evidence, source URL, field verification, contributor confirmation, business-owner claim.
  • Partner with Data Science to develop confidence scores for POI, address, and geofence objects.
F. Troubleshooting, RCA & Product Feedback
  • Investigate user and internal reports related to wrong POI location, missing POI, duplicate POI, incorrect address, wrong category, wrong entrance, bad search result, failed reverse geocode, and incorrect geofence trigger.
  • Conduct root-cause analysis for high-impact POI/search/geocoding incidents.
  • Translate field/user feedback into data updates, search-ranking improvements, or product requirements.
  • Define escalation workflows for P0/P1 location-search issues.
G. Documentation, SOPs & Team Enablement
  • Build and maintain documentation for POI data standard, category taxonomy, address standard, geocoding QA, search quality QA, geofence data standard, contributor validation, and POI release checklist.
  • Train junior MapOps members on POI creation, validation, and QA standards.
  • Work with Product, Engineering, Data Science, and Business teams to shape the POI/Search roadmap.

Requirements

Must-Have

  • 2–4 years of hands-on experience in POI operations, geocoding, local search, GIS data operations, map operations, ride-hailing, logistics, delivery, navigation, or location-based products.
  • Strong understanding of POI data structures, address data, administrative boundaries, and spatial data quality.
  • Experience with QGIS, PostgreSQL/PostGIS, spreadsheets, and spatial QA workflows.
  • Working knowledge of OpenStreetMap data, POI tags, address tags, administrative boundaries, and Overpass API.
  • Strong understanding of search-quality concepts: autocomplete, fuzzy search, category search, ranking, zero-result analysis, and click-through analysis.
  • Experience with POI deduplication, conflation, or data normalization.
  • Strong communication skills with Product, Engineering, Operations, and Business teams.

Nice-to-Have

  • Experience with Elasticsearch, OpenSearch, Pelias, Nominatim, Photon, or similar search/geocoding systems.
  • Experience with Mapbox Search Box API, Google Places API, HERE Geocoding, TomTom Search API, GrabMaps Places, or equivalent APIs.
  • Experience with geofence creation, polygon QA, point-in-polygon analysis, or service-area modeling.
  • Experience with Python for ETL, POI cleaning, deduplication, and spatial analysis.
  • Familiarity with Vietnamese address normalization and administrative boundary changes.
  • Familiarity with mobility, parking, tolling, EV charging, fuel, logistics, insurance, or automotive service data.
  • OSM contribution history or open-source mapping experience.

Soft Skills & Mindset

  • Detail-oriented and skeptical about data quality.
  • Strong ownership of data correctness and user experience.
  • Comfortable with ambiguous, messy, incomplete real-world data.
  • Data-driven mindset; decisions grounded in metrics and evidence.
  • Able to translate business needs into data standards and operational workflows.
  • Able to work independently and lead small data-quality initiatives.
